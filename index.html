About Me

I am a Senior Data Scientist, AI Consultant, and Educator, specializing in Generative AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and deep learning. My expertise lies in designing and deploying cutting-edge AI solutions for decision support, automation, and predictive analytics across industries such as pharmaceuticals, finance, manufacturing, and retail. I have led AI-driven strategic decision-making projects, developed intelligent chatbots and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems, and built scalable machine learning architectures that transform data into actionable insights. My work focuses on making AI explainable, efficient, and impactful for businesses and users alike. As an educator, I teach Machine Learning and Generative AI courses at Panthéon-Sorbonne University, bridging academic knowledge with real-world applications. With a PhD in Statistics, my research background includes time series forecasting, anomaly detection, and AI ethics, ensuring that my AI solutions are not only powerful but also responsible.
